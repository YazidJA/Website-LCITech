About Us.

LCI Tech is a global and decentralized company that focuses on the integration of hardware and software technologies to approach daily challenges and solve them with radical creativity.

Our R&D team is distributed among 3 different laboratories and our Engineering support is active in 6 different countries: United States, Vietnam, Malaysia, China, Netherlands, Argentina and Spain.

Our main task is to design and develop electromechanical prototypes for commercial and non-commercial purposes.

We also take a great interest in Marketing and e-Commerce solutions, mainly in the development and distribution of a new brand or product.
